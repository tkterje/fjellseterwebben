THE ROUTE

The route goes from Lerkendal to Skistua (the ski cabin). The track is 8,1 km with about 420 meter elevation.

There will be busses going down from Skistua.

Se the route in Google Maps.

AN EXPERIENCE

This year our focus is the experience. We're making it more than just a race; a memorable experience.

We and our sponsors are making sure there are prizes and pizza when you finish.

SIGNUP

* The price includes a one-time licence. If you've paid a full year licence you can get a refund on the one-time fee here

This year we are using PayPal. Finish the form, pay, and you're good to go (or run)!

The deadline for signing up has been postponed to saturday 18. april kl 23:59.

PRACTICAL INFORMATION

Fjellseterløpet is arranged 22 april, and is starting at Lerkendal at 18:00.

Påmelding

You can sign up here until saturday at 23:59. Signing up later will result in an additional fee.

Our age classes are: 15-22, 23-39, 40-59 og 60+ (with genders separated). The three best in each class will be rewarded. Also, the best man and woman (regardless of age) will get a price.

Licence

In January (2014) the Norwegian Athletics Federation introduced new rules for licensing. Therefore we have included the one-time license (30nok) in the registration fee. This can be refunded here if you already have a full year licence.
