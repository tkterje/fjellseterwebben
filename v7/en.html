PRACTICAL

Start number

You can get your start number at Løplabbet in the city 16:00-18:00 the day before the race (Tuesday), or at Stripa at Gløshaugen 10:00-12:00, the day of the race.

Start

The race starts at Lerkendal kl 18:00. Meet up there before 17:45.

Classes

Male/female: 12-13, 14-15, 16-17, 18-19, 20-22, 23-34, 35-39, 40-44 (continuing with classes for every 5 yearss).

Baggage

Bags can be delivered at Lerkendal. They will be transported to the top.

Transport

There will be buses going down from the top to Lerkendal.

Studenterhytta

You can go to the student cabin after the race. There, you can shower and get some coffee.

Food

The chef at Studenterhytta (the student cabin) delivers food at the finishline for those who order it. If you have signed up, but not ordered food; order here. There will also be some bananas and sports snacks from Vitagile.

Prizes

There will be prizes for the best runner in each class and to the top 3 male/female overal. More information about prizes will come after the race.

Questions

If you have any questions, send a message to Facebook page (fast response) or send an email kontakt@fjellseterlopet.no.

TRADITION

The race was originally named Sigmonds Staup. It has been a tradition among students for years, and the same route is still used after some NTNUI meetings. In 1987 Fjellseterløpet was established as a successor to the original Staup.
