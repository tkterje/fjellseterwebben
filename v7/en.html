THE RACE

The race starts at Lerkendal, and goes over the Stavne bridge, then under the train tracks and some meters along Osloveien. The route then goes to the left along Mørlenda, under the tram line, through the roundabout at Breidablikk and up Breidablikkveien to Byåsen shopping center. Then it follows Fjellseterveien all the way to the goal at Skistua. The route is 8.1 km with an incline of 420 meters.

TRADITION

The race was originally named Sigmonds Staup. It has been a tradition among students for years, and the same route is still used after some NTNUI meetings. In 1987 Fjellseterløpet was established as a successor to the original Staup.
