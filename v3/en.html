For everyone!

Wednesday 22. of april is the day Fjellseterløpet kicks of! Run in your own pace. Eat pizza at the finish line. This is an event for everyone.

Norsk

THE ROUTE

The route goes from Lerkendal to Skistua (the ski cabin). The track is 8,1 km with about 420 meter elevation.

There will be busses going down from Skistua.

Se the route in Google Maps.

AN EXPERIENCE

This year we focus on the experience. We're making it more than just a race; a memorable experience.

We and our sponsors are making sure there are prizes and pizza when you finish.

Join our Instagram competition and win cool prices! The winners will be announced at our Facebook page.

Signup

You can sign up for an additional fee on the day of the race at Gløshaugen. Signup hours: 10:00 - 12:00 ("Stripa" Gløshaugen) and 15:00 - 17:00 (Lerkendal).

To get your license refund (if you have a full year license) click here.

Ordinary signup is no longer possible.

PRACTICAL INFORMATION

Fjellseterløpet is arranged 22 april, and is starting at Lerkendal at 18:00.

Signup and startnumbers

You can sign up for an additional fee on the day of the race at Gløshaugen. Signup hours: 10:00 - 12:00 ("Stripa" Gløshaugen) and 15:00 - 17:00 (Lerkendal).

Startnumbers can be picked up at 10:00 - 12:00 ("Stripa" Gløshaugen) and 15:00 - 17:30 (Lerkendal).

Our age classes are: 15-22, 23-39, 40-59 og 60+ (with genders separated). The three best in each class will be rewarded. Also, the best man and woman (regardless of age) will get a price.

Baggage og transport

We will transport baggage from Lerkendal before the race and we will have buses going down from the top.

Licence

In January (2014) the Norwegian Athletics Federation introduced new rules for licensing. Therefore we have included the one-time license (30nok) in the registration fee. This can be refunded here if you already have a full year licence.

Questions

If you have any questions please send a message to our facebook page or send an email to kontakt@fjellseterlopet.no .
